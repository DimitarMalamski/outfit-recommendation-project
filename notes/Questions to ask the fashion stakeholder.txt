Questions to ask the fashion stakeholder:

1. How do you decide if clothing items go well together?
2. What makes two items compatible rather than just visually similar?
3. Are there any basic rules people follow when combining outfits?
4. How important is context (like occasion or setting) when choosing outfits?
5. If you had to judge an outfit recommendation, what would make it “good” or “bad”?
6. Do you think starting from one item and recommending others is realistic?
7. What are the biggest mistakes people make when combining outfits?

Questions to ask the regular user stakeholder:

1. How do you usually decide what clothes go well together?
2. Do you ever find it difficult to match outfits? If yes, what makes it difficult?
3. When you see an outfit, what makes you think it looks good?
4. Would you use a system that suggests outfits based on one item? Why or why not?
5. What would make you trust or not trust such recommendations?
6. Would you prefer simple suggestions (just matching items) or more detailed ones (based on style, occasion, etc.)?
