# Dataset Labeling Rules

## Styles

**Casual** - Everyday relaxed clothing. Simple, wearable, and neutral. Not formal, not clearly sporty, and not strongly streetwear.

**Formal** -Polished and structured clothing. Suitable for office, events, or dressier situations. Often cleaner and more refined in appearance.

**Sporty** -Athletic or activewear-inspired clothing. Items designed for sport or strongly associated with exercise and performance wear.

**Streetwear** -Urban, trend-driven clothing with stronger visual identity. May include logos, graphic prints, oversized cuts, or bold styling elements.

## Types

**Tshirts** - Upper-body clothing such as t-shirts, shirts, polos, and sweaters.

**Pants** - Lower-body clothing such as jeans, trousers, joggers, and leggings.

**Shoes** - Footwear such as sneakers, trainers, and dress shoes.

**Jackets** - Outer-layer clothing such as jackets, blazers, coats, and zip jackets.

## Rules

1. Keep only images that clearly fit one folder.
2. If an item is too colourful or contains prints, label is as _streetwear_.
3. If an item has no strong formal, sporty, or streetwear signal, label it as _casual_.
4. Type is based on the clothing category itself, not on brand or styling details.
5. Style is based on visual appearance, not only on the website category.
